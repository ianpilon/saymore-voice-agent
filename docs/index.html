The interview

Like a great host,
not a survey.

  • One question at a time. Never a survey, never a checklist of boxes to tick.
  • It reacts before it asks. “Oh, that’s wild” — then the next question. A conversation, not an interrogation.
  • It chases specifics. “Give me the actual example.” The first answer is always the generic one; the gold is in the follow-up.
  • It never tries to make you sound polished. Real beats impressive. The post-worthiness takes care of itself later.
  • It knows when to stop. Over-interviewing kills the magic. When there’s enough, it wraps.

Saymore is a thinking partner for finding the things worth saying — not a therapist.
